1. I read seven books, I liked them all. .

Which one of the FANBOYS can be added after the comma to fix this? And What can we replace the comma with to fix this? . or ;
What is the purpose of a semicolon? To connect two independent clauses with similar ideas together without a conjunction.

2. I love steak; with pepper and salt!

This is incorrect because “with pepper and salt!” is a dependent clause, semicolons are used with independent clauses. Additionally, semicolons have other purposes like listing.
Example: John shows his spoiled nature in many instances: he threw his new toys; he spat out his salmon; and he broke the expensive vase.
